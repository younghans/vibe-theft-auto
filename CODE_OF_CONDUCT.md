# Code Of Conduct

We want Vibe Theft Auto to be a welcoming place for players, contributors, and
curious builders.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps the project:

- Be kind, patient, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming problems clearly.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Respect different levels of experience with game development, JavaScript,
  networking, 3D assets, and open source.

Examples of behavior that does not belong here:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without permission.
- Trolling, repeated off-topic disruption, or bad-faith issue spam.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block accounts, or take
other reasonable action when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainer privately. For
security vulnerabilities, follow `SECURITY.md`.
