Vibe Hero classical MP3 sources

Vibe Hero streams these original MP3 source URLs and plays only the snippet
window defined in src/shared/vibeHero.js. The MP3 files are not bundled locally
so the web build stays inside its existing runtime asset budget.

Debussy - Arabesque No. 1
- Work: Claude Debussy, Arabesque No. 1, L. 66
- Performer/source credit: Gregor Quendel / Classicals.de
- Source page: https://www.classicals.de/debussy-arabesques
- Original MP3 source: https://library.classicalmusicarchive.org/music/GQ%20Collection/Classicals.de%20-%20Debussy%20-%20Arabesque%20No.%201%20-%20L.%2066.mp3
- Snippet window: 0:00-1:15, charted from substantial source-MP3 piano onsets.
- Notes: The composition is public domain in the United States. Classicals.de
  publishes the recording with attribution requirements; Gregor Quendel also
  publishes this recording on Pixabay as "Debussy - Arabesque No. 1 - L 66" for
  use under the Pixabay Content License.
